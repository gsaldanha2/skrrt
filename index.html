How To Play

  • Consume gas cans or hit green tailed cars to gain points
  • Don't run out of gas!
  • Avoid cars with red tails - they can destroy you
  • Avoid wreckage when people die
  • Shift lanes or turn at an intersection with the arrow keys or by swiping (mobile)
  • Slow down using spacebar or the slow button (mobile)
  • Get to the top of the leaderboard!
